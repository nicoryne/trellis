# Trellis V2 vision

The mature-product surface of [[trellis|Trellis]]. Documented for vision only; not on the build path until after V1 lands paying customers.

## Synthesis

V2 turns Trellis from a per-firm knowledge platform into an **industry-spanning intelligence layer**. Cross-firm anonymized benchmarks become possible because every firm's team graph shares the same schema. Practice-area expansion takes the product beyond litigation. EU deployment unlocks a non-trivial fraction of global SAM. Light theme arrives, finally, because some firms ask for it.

## What ships in V2

- **Cross-firm anonymized benchmark intelligence** — practice-area patterns across firms, without exposing any one firm's data.
- **Advanced analytics dashboards** for KM leadership (cohort adoption, knowledge coverage, time-to-productivity).
- **Ethical wall automation** — inherits from connected systems, surfaces conflicts before retrieval returns a node.
- **Slack / Teams capture and notifications** (graduating from V1's "Important" tier into mature surface).
- **Westlaw / LexisNexis browser extension** for research capture.
- **Zoom / Teams meeting transcript ingestion**.
- **Expansion beyond litigation** to corporate, IP, regulatory practice groups.
- **SOC 2 Type II completed**, ISO 27001, **GDPR-ready, EU deployment regions**.
- **Light theme** (V1 is dark-only).
- **Onboarding tour**, settings/preferences page, account switching UI.
- **Search across personal + team graphs simultaneously** — addresses the [[attorney-client-privilege|privilege]] handling question that V1 leaves open.

## Why V2 is documented now

Investors and KM Partners ask "what's the trajectory beyond the first contract?" V2 makes the answer specific. It also clarifies what is **not** Trellis — the [[trellis-product-requirements]] "OUT" list (built-in drafting, time tracking, conflict-checking automation, client portals, court-filing integration) explicitly fences the product against legal-tech expansion into adjacent commodity workflows.

## Tensions

- **Cross-firm benchmark intelligence** is the V2 commercial unlock — but requires customer trust that anonymization is intact, and a critical mass of firms on Trellis.
- **GDPR / EU regions**: per-region deployment cost is non-trivial; what's the threshold of EU customer demand that triggers it?

## Open questions

- Will Trellis expand to non-firm legal departments (in-house counsel) before or after practice-area expansion within firms?
