# What is the documented capture-frequency target for litigators?

## Status

**Open** — awaiting Keith's answer.

## Why it matters

The [[adoption-strategy]] depends on lawyers actually using the personal brain. The PRD describes capture surfaces but does not commit to a per-week minimum. Without a target, there is no leading indicator that adoption is on track or off — and the team graph stays sparse if capture stays sparse. (see [[litigator]])

## What we know so far

- Three capture surfaces (text, audio, image).
- Lawyers bill 1,900+ hours/year and resist documentation friction.
- No target capture frequency named.

## What would resolve it

- A reasonable target (e.g. "3–5 captures per active matter per week" or "1 capture per active workday").
- A metric to track in [[trellis-product-requirements|user-level metrics]] (V1+).
- Honest framing: if the target isn't hit, adoption-strategy Pillar 1 has failed and the product needs rework.
