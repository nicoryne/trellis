# V2 expansion — in-house counsel first, or other practice areas first?

## Status

**Open** — awaiting Keith's answer.

## Why it matters

The [[trellis-v2-vision|V2 roadmap]] lists both "expansion beyond litigation to corporate, IP, regulatory practice groups" and "in-house legal teams at large corporations" as expansion vectors. They sell to different buyers, demand different positioning, and require different product investments. Doing both at once dilutes focus. (see [[trellis-v2-vision]])

## What we know so far

- Other practice groups within target firms: same firm, same buyer, additive ACV.
- In-house teams: different buyer (General Counsel / Chief Legal Officer), different sales motion, different schema needs (regulatory matters, transactional knowledge).

## What would resolve it

- Pick which expansion direction is V2-near vs V2-far.
- Articulate why (likely: other practice groups first because expansion within existing customers is cheaper than net-new sales).
- Update the V2 roadmap to make the order explicit.
