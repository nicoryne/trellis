# Notion

Generic team knowledge/workspace product. Appears twice in the Trellis material:

1. As a **competitor** in [[trellis-product-brief|the competitive matrix]] — generic team knowledge tool that Trellis differentiates against by being **purpose-built for legal**: privilege protection, ethical walls, audit logs, citation-grounded retrieval.
2. As a **brand reference** in [[trellis-design-guidelines]] (#2 of three priorities) for "content density, structured editing, and warm professional feel."

## Key facts

- **Category**: generic team knowledge / workspace.
- **Trellis differentiator**: legal compliance built in from day one; Notion has none of it natively.
- **Design influence**: structured editing and density patterns inform the [[trellis-design-system|Trellis design system]].
- **Adjacent**: Confluence (legacy enterprise), Roam, [[obsidian]] (personal KM).
